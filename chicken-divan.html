Chicken Divan

Description

A yummy Indian inspired, creamy, chicken broccoli and cheese dish!

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken tenderloins
  • 2 cans of cream of mushroom or broccoli soup
  • 1 bag frozen broccoli
  • 1 bag shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup white rice
  • Cumin

Steps

  1. Boil chicken breast tenderloins
  2. Chop chicken breasts into bite size pieces
  3. Empty 2 cans of cream of mushroom/broccoli soup into a mixing bowl
  4. Mix the chicken into the bowl of cream of mushroom/broccoli soup
  5. Shake cumin on top and mix well, more for spicier result
  6. Scoop chicken, cream, cheese and cumin mixture into a casserole dish
  7. Microwave the bag of broccoli
  8. Mix the broccoli into the casserole dish
  9. Layer shredded cheddar cheese on top
  10. Bake in oven at 425 degrees or until cheese is bubbling
  11. While dish is in the oven, boil 1 cup of rice
  12. Serve dish on top of rice
