Pot Roast

Description

Easy slow cooker pot roast with potatoes, carrots and onions

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Roast beef
  • clove of garlic or garlic powder
  • 2 bags of chopped carrots
  • sliced and diced onion
  • 1/2 lb of sliced potatoes

Steps

  1. dice garlic or shake garlic powder on to roast beef
  2. slice potatoes
  3. put roast beef in slow cooker
  4. Add the potatoes and the carrots
  5. Add water to just cover the roast
  6. Cook on low all day
  7. Enjoy
