Spaghetti

Description

Simple 20 minute Spaghetti with meat sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of hamburger meat
  • Ragu spaghetti sauce of choice
  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook hamburger meat in skillet until brown
  2. Add in Ragu sauce and cook to simmer
  3. While meat sauce simmering, boil spaghetti noodles until tender
  4. Serve meat sauce over noodles
  5. Add parmesan cheese to taste
